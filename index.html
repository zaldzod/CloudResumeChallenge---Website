About

Chizalum Echeta is a devops/cloud engineer, cybersecurity and crypto enthusiast. Prior experience in networking and business operations inform his mindful approach to building competitively priced, well designed and secure cloud infrastructure systems. Having an advanced computer science degree ensures that he fully understands computing fundamentals. He considers himself an ‘ongoing student’, which really means that he leverages on this knowledge to keep in tune with the latest technological stacks through continued MOOCs, certifications and trainings.

Chizalum finds the idea of being a multicloud engineer interesting, consequently, he has pursued that strategy and achieved 'multiicloud fluency’ in two of the three major US cloud providers – Google Cloud and Microsoft Azure. Quite recently, he completed the Professional cloud architect training on Oracle University. On-topic with fluency, Chizalum has upper intermediate language skills in Mandarin. While he considers this one of his most daunting learning experiences ever, it was a wholesome and worthy challenge that culminated in an ability to fit in and work with diverse, multicultural teams.

Chizalum employs strategic time, orginazational and project management methods to help drive productivity alongside a healthy work-life balance. He believes collaboration and communication are crucial soft skills and pursues various hobbies such as meditation, crypto research, chess and yoga.

Chizalum is currently in the process of building a career as a multi cloud specialist, and is looking to take on challenging cloud/devops engineering roles which offer growth opportunities. Feel free to reach me with contact details below to connect!
